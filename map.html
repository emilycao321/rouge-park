About

The Rouge Park Interactive Map is a project through the U-M Public Design Corp, a University of Michigan Taubman College of Architecture and Urban Planning program offering University of Michigan students the opportunity to engage in socially-driven design.


The University of Michigan's Public Design Corps (U-M PDC) is made possible through the support and mentorship of leading faculty from Taubman College of Architecture and Urban Planning. Mentors bring a breadth of international and local expertise in socially-driven design projects and assist student teams in navigating complex design challenges.
